BERSERK BOLOGNESE

PASTA MADE WITH LAUGHTER AND JOY

This pasta recipe is 500 years old and passed down the Coborlwitz family for generations.

Make sure to make a lot so your mom can store it in the freezer for future use when she is too tired to make food.

INGREDIENTS:

  • MEAT
  • OLIVE OIL
  • CARROTS
  • CELERY
  • ONIONS
  • SEASONING & STUFF
  • pasta and stuff

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cut the vegtables
  3. Add them to the meat
  4. Seasoning
  5. Boil the pasta
  6. put the meat on the pasta on low heat when it is done
  7. serve
